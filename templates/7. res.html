Oatmeal porridge with strawberries

This oatmeal porridge with strawberries is a real morning magic!

How to cook

Ingredients:

Oat flakes - 75 grams

Oat milk - 250 milliliters

Strawberries - 100 grams (80 grams for sauce, 20 grams for decoration)

Dates - 5 pieces

Vanilla syrup - 1-2 drops

First step

1. Prepare all ingredients.

Second step

2. Put a saucepan with oat milk on medium heat, bring to a boil.

Third step

3. Add oat flakes and vanilla syrup, cook porridge for 10 minutes.

Fourth step

4. Grind strawberries and dates with a blender to a homogeneous puree.

Fifth step

5. Put the porridge on a plate, pour the strawberry sauce on top and add a couple of chopped strawberries.

Sixth step

6. Oatmeal porridge with strawberries is ready, bon appetit!
