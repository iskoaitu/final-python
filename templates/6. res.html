Fried rice with chicken

Everyone at home will like these nuggets; in addition, they are much tastier than store-bought ones, since they turn out much juicier and softer.

How to cook

Ingredients:

Fig - 1 cup

Chicken fillet - 300 g

Onion - 1 piece

Garlic - 2 cloves

Carrot - 1 piece

Green peas - 100 g (Can be frozen.)

Egg - 2 pcs

Soy sauce - 2 tablespoons

Salt - to taste

Ground black pepper - to taste

First step

1.Cook the rice. Set aside.

Second step

2. Cut the chicken fillet into small pieces, equal in size

Third step

3. Fry chicken fillet pieces in vegetable oil on all sides.

Fourth step

4.Finely chop the onion and garlic.

Fifth step

5.Cut the carrots into cubes, small. Mix with green peas and add garlic and onion. stir the vegetables.

Sixth step

6. First, fry the vegetables a little in a frying pan. Until soft. Then spread them into a ring shape on the sides of the pan.

Seventh step

7.Beat the eggs in a cup, then pour them into the middle of the ring

Eighth step

8. When the omelet is a little cooked, mix the vegetables with the egg and pour in the rice.

Ninth step

9.Add the fried chicken, then soy sauce and stir.

Tenth step

10. Fry everything together until the rice begins to brown. And now, your fried rice with chicken is ready.
