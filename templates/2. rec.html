Pizza

This pizza will appeal to everyone at home; in addition, they are prepared much faster than store-bought and in a restaurant, as they turn out much juicier and tastier.

How to cook

Ingredients :

Wheat flour - 300 grams

Warm water - 300 grams

Vegetable oil - 3 tablespoons

Dry yeast - 1 teaspoon

Sugar - 1 teaspoon

Salt - 1 pinch

Pepperoni sausage - 200 grams

Mozzarella - 300 grams

Tomato sauce - 4 tablespoons

First step

Prepare all the necessary ingredients. Take the mozzarella that is more often on sale.

Second step

Sift flour into a deep bowl, add sugar, salt, butter and yeast, pour in warm water.

Third step

Mix all the ingredients first with a spoon.

Fourth step

Then knead the dough with your hands, if necessary, add a little flour, the dough should stick to your hands a little. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and leave for 30 minutes in a warm place.

Fifth step

In the meantime, slice the sausage thinly and chop the mozzarella in any convenient way.

Sixth step

The dough that has come up needs to be kneaded and divided into two parts, you will get two pizzas with a diameter of 30 centimeters. Roll out thinly on parchment, add flour if necessary. Brush the dough with your favorite tomato sauce.

Seventh step

Generously sprinkle a layer of tomato sauce with chopped mozzarella.

Eighth step

Arrange the pepperoni slices on top. Place in a preheated oven to 200 degrees for 15 minutes in the lower and upper heating mode.

Ninth step

Serve the finished pizza to the table hot, sliced into slices with a sharp knife.

Last step

Pizza with pepperoni and mozzarella is ready. Enjoy your meal.
