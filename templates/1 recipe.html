Nuggets like McDonald's

Everyone at home will like these nuggets; in addition, they are much tastier than store-bought ones, since they turn out much juicier and softer.

How to cook

Ingredients:

Chicken fillet - 300 g

Chicken egg - 2 pcs.

White loaf - 100 g

Salt and pepper - to taste

Breadcrumbs - 150 g

Vegetable oil - for frying

First step

1. If the fillet is from frozen, defrost and dry it.

Second step

2. Cut the loaf into cubes, put it in a blender, and grind it into crumbs.

Third step

3. Next, cut the chicken fillet, punch it with a blender into a homogeneous minced meat.

Fourth step

4. Pour bread crumbs into a deep bowl and add minced chicken.

Fifth step

5. Add salt and pepper to taste, beat in a small egg.

Sixth step

6. Knead the minced meat thoroughly until smooth.

Seventh step

7. Line a cutting board with foil, with wet hands form small pieces in the form of classic nuggets, place them on foil, and place in the freezer for 15-20 minutes.

Eighth step

8. In the meantime, beat the remaining egg until light foam. Dip nuggets in it.

Ninth step

9.Dip them in breadcrumbs.

Tenth step

10. And fry in a small amount of vegetable oil until golden brown.

Eleventh step

11. Cooking time will depend on the height of your preparations, but you should not overdry them.

Twelfth step

12. The finished nuggets will even be similar in cut to the original, but juicier and tastier.
