Ramen in the style of Naruto

After cooking this ramen, I felt like a real ninja from Konoha!

How to cook

Ingredients:

Ramen noodles – 1 pack

Chicken - 750 g

Eggs - 2 pcs.

Carrot - 80 g

Onion - 80 g

Garlic - 3 cloves

Soy sauce - 4 tbsp.

Ginger powder - 0.5 tsp.

Chili pepper - 3 pcs.

Green onion - 10 g

Sesame seeds - 1 tsp.

Water - 1 l

Noodles - 200 g

Whole allspice - 3 pcs.

First step

1. Prepare all the ingredients for the soup. If you have a frozen chicken, then take it out of the freezer in advance and defrost it. This time I used two small chicken legs at the rate of one ham per serving. Wash the eggs, boil them hard and peel them.

Second step

2.Wash the chicken, remove the remaining feathers, if any. Put the meat in a saucepan, pour cold water and set it on the stove. Bring to a boil over high heat. How to choose a soup pot, read a separate article at the end of the recipe.

Third step

3.At this time, peel the carrots, onions and garlic. Rinse the vegetables.

Fourth step

4. Carefully remove the formed foam in the pan. Send the carrots and onions to the broth, reduce the flame to moderate. Cook the chicken until cooked under a half-closed lid. It took me about 30-35 minutes.

Fifth step

5. Remove the finished meat from the pan, strain the broth. Cut the slightly cooled chicken into small pieces.

Sixth step

6.Pour soy sauce into the chicken broth, add chopped garlic, allspice, ginger powder and chili pepper. Stir and taste for salt. If necessary, add salt to taste. Return the pan to the stove and leave it to heat up at the lowest heat.

Seventh step

7.Boil the noodles in a separate saucepan

Eighth step

8.Take a deep plate. Put the boiled noodles closer to one edge, as in the photo.

Ninth step

9. Place the chicken pieces on the other half. Cut the egg in half and send it to a plate. At this stage, you can add fresh soy sprouts. I used pickled vegetables for Asian soups. Pour in the hot broth.

Tenth step

10.Sprinkle with sesame seeds and green onions, chopped finely.

Last step

ramen with chicken is ready, you can serve
