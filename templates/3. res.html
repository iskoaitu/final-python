burger

The best homemade burger

How to cook

Ingredients:

Ground beef - 500-600 grams

Tomatoes - 2 pieces

Pickled cucumber - 3-4 pieces

Onion - 2 pieces

Garlic - 1-2 cloves (optional)

Buns - 4-5 pieces

Ketchup - 3-4 tablespoons

Mustard - 1 tablespoon

Cheese - 4-5 slices

Vegetable oil - 1-2 tablespoons

Salt - 0.5 teaspoons

Ground black pepper - 2 pinches

Egg - 1 piece

First step

1. Add salt, pepper, 2/3 finely chopped onion, chopped garlic and yolk to the ground beef. Stir thoroughly

Second step

2.Divide the minced meat into 4-5 parts and form a flat round cutlet from each. In the middle we will make a small indentation with our thumb.

Third step

3. Put the cutlets on a flat plate and send them to the refrigerator for 20 minutes.

Fourth step

4. And now fry the burger patties well in vegetable oil until cooked. It can be baked in the oven at 180 degrees or grilled.

Fifth step

5. We cut the burger buns in half and fry them on the grill, in a dry frying pan or in a toaster.

Sixth step

6. Lubricate one half of the bun with ketchup, and the other with mustard or mustard sauce.

Seventh step

7. Thoroughly rinse the lettuce leaves, and then dry. We spread lettuce leaves on the bottom of the bun, on top of it — slices of pickled cucumber.

Eighth step

8. We spread the cutlet.

Ninth step

9.Cover the hot cutlet with a slice of cheese, lay out slices of tomatoes and onion rings. Onions can be taken raw, or fried.

Tenth step

10.And cover with the second part of the bun. Enjoy your meal!
