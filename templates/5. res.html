pasta

This recipe has turned my kitchen into an Italian trattoria!

How to cook

Ingredients:

Pasta (pasta) - 250 g

Chicken fillet - 1 pc. (medium size)

Bacon - 3 slices (about 50 g)

Cream (15-20%) - 300 ml

Onion - 1 pc. (small size)

Hard cheese - 50 g

Vegetable oil - 2-3 tablespoons

Wheat flour - 1 teaspoon

Garlic - 1 clove

Basil (fresh) - optional

Salt - to taste

Ground black pepper - to taste

First step

1. Prepare the ingredients for pasta with chicken in cream sauce. First of all, put a pot of water on the fire (at least 3 liters of water) - for cooking pasta, as well as a deep frying pan - for making sauce.

Second step

2. Remove the skin from the chicken fillet and cut it into small pieces.

Third step

3.Cut the bacon slices into medium cubes, cut a small onion into smaller cubes, and pass a clove of garlic through a press or also cut very finely with a knife.

Fourth step

4. Pour 1-2 tablespoons of oil into a preheated frying pan, put the chicken fillet cubes and fry them over high heat, stirring, for 1-2 minutes. If the frying pan is small, then it is better to fry the chicken in portions so that the pieces do not start to let the juice. Transfer the fried fillet to a plate.

Fifth step

5. Put the bacon in a frying pan and fry it until browned. If a lot of fat has been released from the bacon during frying, remove it with a spoon so that the dish does not get too greasy. Add the onion to the fried bacon and fry them together for 1 minute. You do not need to blush the onion, it should only become transparent.

Sixth step

6. Add the chicken fillet pieces, mix and pour out the flour. While stirring, warm everything together for 1 minute.

Seventh step

7. Pour in the cream and cook over low heat until the sauce thickens.

Eighth step

8. Grate the cheese on a fine grater and add it to the creamy sauce with chicken (part of the cheese can be left for serving). Add garlic and season the cream sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

Ninth step

9.As soon as the water boils, add salt to it (about 1 tbsp. l.) and lay out the pasta. Cook according to the instructions. Be careful and don't overcook the pasta!

Tenth step

10. We catch the finished pasta with tongs (or a slotted spoon) and transfer it to a frying pan with sauce. Stir and warm up for a minute. If the sauce is thick and lies on the pasta in a thick layer, add a little water from the pan in which the pasta was cooked to the pan until the desired density is obtained.

Last step

11.Serve pasta with chicken in cream sauce immediately after cooking! Before serving, each serving can be sprinkled with fresh basil leaves, as well as grated cheese. Enjoy your meal!
