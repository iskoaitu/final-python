Pancakes

It turned out incredibly airy and fragrant – like a real breakfast of champions

How to cook

Ingredients:

Chicken eggs - 3 pieces

Milk - 140 milliliters

Wheat flour - 115 grams

Baking powder - 1 teaspoon

Salt - 1 pinch

First step

1.Prepare all the necessary ingredients.

Second step

2. Separate the yolks from the whites. Add salt to the yolks.

Third step

3. Pour in the milk at room temperature, mix with the yolks with a whisk.

Fourth step

4.Add the sifted flour with baking powder.

Fifth step

5. Stir until smooth and smooth dough.

Sixth step

6. Beat the egg whites to a steady state.

Seventh step

7. Put the egg whites into the dough in parts and mix gently. It turns out an airy dough. Immediately start baking.

Sixth step

8. Heat the pan well, you can grease it with a drop of oil just before baking the first batch of pancakes. Spoon out the dough, fry at low heat for 1.5-2 minutes.

Seventh step

9. Gently turn over with a spatula and fry for another 30-40 seconds on the second side.

Sixth step

10. I will offer some ideas for a delicious filling. You can sprinkle the dough with blueberries or other berries, crispy toasted bacon or pancetta, banana or grated chocolate. I used chocolate drops and berries. Fry like pancakes without filling.

Seventh step

11. Stack the ready-made pancakes, pour honey, maple syrup and serve with berries.

Sixth step

12. The pancakes are incredibly tender and delicious! Enjoy your meal!
