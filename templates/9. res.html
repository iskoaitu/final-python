Colored cupcakes

The perfect balance of sweetness and freshness. Great for a cozy tea party or as a dessert for a holiday

How to cook

Ingredients:

Flour - 1 cup

Butter - 50 grams

Sugar - 75 grams

Sour cream - 75 milliliters

Egg - 1 piece

Soda - 0.5 teaspoons

First step

1. Prepare all ingredients.

Second step

2. In a deep bowl, beat the egg. Add sour cream and sugar.

Third step

3. Whisk everything together and add the soft butter.

Fourth step

4. Whisk everything again. Add flour and baking soda. Mix everything until smooth and divide the dough into three or more parts, depending on how many colors you will use.

Fifth step

5.Add a few drops of gel food coloring to each part of the dough. Mix well so that the dye is completely mixed with the dough. I got three colors: blue, green and crimson.

Sixth step

6.Spread the dough on the molds, filling them about 2/3. First, a teaspoon of one color, then another, etc. Bake the cupcakes in the oven at 200 degrees for about 25 minutes.

Seventh step

7.Cupcakes are very light and airy! Enjoy your meal! Advice from Isco: You can check the cupcakes with a wooden splinter. It should be dry and without pieces of dough.
